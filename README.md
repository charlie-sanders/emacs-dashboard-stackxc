# dashboard-extras

Choose from

* Reddit /r/emacs feed
* Stackexchanges emacs.sx.com 

Customize your dashboard in your .emacs file, example : 
```lisp
(setq dashboard-items '(
   (reddits . 5)
   (stackxc . 5)
   (recents  . 5)
   (bookmarks . 5)
   (projects . 5)))
```
